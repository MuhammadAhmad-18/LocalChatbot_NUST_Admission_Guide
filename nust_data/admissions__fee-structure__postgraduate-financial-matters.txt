### Payment of Student Fees:
  * At the time of admission students have to pay the **admission processing fee, security and full semester fee in advance**. For MBA, MS HRM, EMBA, MS Marketing, MS Logistics & Supply Chain Management & MS Innovation & Entrepreneurship, HBS Licencing Fee @ Rs.22,000/year would be included in the invoice at the time of admission.
  * Invoice/Challan for admission charges along with first semester fee and Provisional Selection Letter will be sent through email/ available on NUST website.
  * Tuition fee will be payable on semester basis. Students have to pay their fee in advance before the commencement of semester otherwise they will not be eligible to sit in the class.
  * Student will be charged full tuition fee for 4 regular semesters (2 academic years). After four semesters, if he/she continues study/research work he/she will be charged Rs. 10,000/- per semester Bench fee (registration maintenance fee) till completion of degree requirements. Furthermore, a student will also be required to pay the prescribed course fee @ Rs. 8,000/- per credit hour for deficient / improvement/additional courses (if any). Fee during Summer Semester will be credit hours based @ Rs. 8,000/- per credit hour. If a student completes the degree requirements with in 30 days from commencement of 4th semester, s/he has to pay fee for three regular semesters only (i.e., Fall, Spring & Fall).
  * Summer Semester fee will be charged @ PKR 8,000 per credit hour for all programs less EMBA for which Fee will be charged on the basis of no of courses selected for Summer Semester @ Rs. 68,000/- per course.
  * PhD students will be charged full tuition fee for 3 academic years (6 regular semesters). After six semesters, he/she will be charged Rs. 10,000/- Bench fee (registration maintenance fee) till completion of degree requirements. Furthermore, a student will also be required to pay the prescribed course fee for deficient / improvement /additional courses (if any) @ PKR 8,000 Per credit hour.
### EMBA Students
  * Second week of the semester will be an add/drop period without any financial implications for the students. If the student drops a course after the second week he will be charged full fee for the course and letter grade ‘W’ will be awarded
**Note: All fees and other charges are subject to revision from time to time.**
