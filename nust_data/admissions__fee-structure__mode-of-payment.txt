[__](https://nust.edu.pk)__[Admissions](https://nust.edu.pk/admissions) __[Fee Structure](https://nust.edu.pk/admissions/fee-structure) __MODE OF PAYMENT
You can pay your SEMESTER FEE through following modes:
  1. Using 1-Link mode, accessing through any Bank’s Mobile App.
  2. Over the counter cash deposit **using 1-Bill invoice #** at online branch of any bank.
  3. HBL/Askari Bank online branches (over the counter).
