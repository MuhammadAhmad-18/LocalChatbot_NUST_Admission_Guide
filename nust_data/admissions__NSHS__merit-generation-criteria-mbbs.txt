##### **For Open Merit Seats**
**Qualification** | **Weightage**
---|---
**NUMS MDCAT** | **50%**
**HSSC / A level / Equivalent** | **40%**
**SSC / O level / Equivalent** | **10%**
##### **For Foreign Seats**
**Qualification** | **Weightage**
---|---
**NUMS MDCAT * / SAT-II / MCAT / UCAT** | **50%**
**HSSC / A level / Equivalent** | **40%**
**SSC / O level / Equivalent** | **10%**
* NUMS MDCAT is not mandatory for foreign students rather they can apply based on SAT-II or MCAT or UCAT score.
